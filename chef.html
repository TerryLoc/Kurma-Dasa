Culinary Career

Kurma Dasa's culinary career spans several decades, during which he has authored numerous cookbooks and hosted the popular cooking show "Cooking with Kurma." His recipes, rooted in vegetarian principles, have inspired many to adopt a healthier, more compassionate lifestyle.
